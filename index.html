Privacy Policy

We believe in transparency. QuickTrack is built to protect your privacy while helping you stay organized.

Last updated: 27 Nov 2025

1. Information We Collect

The App does NOT collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII).

The App may process the following (depending on features):

  • Text entered by the user to generate responses (sent to external APIs).
  • Basic, non-identifiable app usage data (app opens, button clicks).

No names, emails, phone numbers, or sensitive personal data are collected.

2. How We Use Information

Any information entered in the App is used only to provide app functionality, such as:

  • Generating automated responses
  • Improving app experience

We do not sell or share data with third parties for marketing.

3. Third-Party Services

The App may use the following external services:

  • 1 OpenAI / Gemini / other third-party APIs (for generating responses)
  • These services may receive text you enter, only for processing.
  • Each service follows its own privacy policy.

4. Data Storage

The App does not store any user data on our servers.

All data stays on the user’s device or is used temporarily by third-party APIs.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13.

We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Any changes will be posted on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this policy, please do not hesitate to reach out.
